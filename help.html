Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
Go to the Login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email and follow the reset link sent to your inbox.
How do I enroll in a course?
After logging in, browse the Courses section, click on your desired course and hit "Enroll Now". Enrollment is instant.
Can I access courses on mobile?
Yes! Student Portal is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets.
How do I track my progress?
Visit your Dashboard after login. You will see progress bars for each enrolled course and assignment completion status.
How do I update my profile?
Click on your profile avatar in the top right corner and select "Edit Profile" to update your information.
What browsers are supported?
We support all modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari. Keep your browser updated for the best experience.
